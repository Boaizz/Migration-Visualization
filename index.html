Migration In Australia

About Us

The goal of this project is to visualize Australia's immigration status, including the country's net overseas migration from 2005 to 2022, which is broken down by visa type, internal migration within Australian states, and the comparison of the number of immigrants coming between Australia and other countries.

For this project, three visualizations have been created. The first visualization shows the total number of arrivals to Australia and is broken down by group type of visa using a choropleth map and a pie chart. The second visualization shows the internal migration between Australian states using a Sankey chart. The final visualization is a bubble chart that shows the comparison of Australia's immigration rates with those of other countries using various statistics.

We hope that our audience will learn new facts regarding the migration pattern in Australia through the visualizations. If you have any comments or recommendations regarding this website, we invite you to get in touch with us.
